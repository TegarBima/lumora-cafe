Our Story

Crafted with Purpose,
Served with Care

Lumora Café was born from a simple belief: great coffee changes conversations. Founded in 2019 in the heart of Jakarta Selatan, we've spent years building relationships with local farmers from Aceh to Flores to bring you Indonesia's finest specialty beans.

Every cup we serve is a result of meticulous sourcing, careful roasting, and skilled barista craft. We don't just serve coffee — we share a piece of the land, the people, and the story behind it.
